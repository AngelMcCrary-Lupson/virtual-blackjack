BLACKJACK

Welcome to the game of Blackjack (aka 21)! Here are the basic rules of this version that you need to know to play.

  • The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

  • Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand. All others are their actual value.

  • The player starts with two cards, the dealer's cards are hidden until the end.

  • To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.

  • If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.

  • If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you've got a blackjack.

  • Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher.

  • Doubling is not allowed. (don't worry if you don't know what this is)

  • You will always bet 5 credits to play each hand, and if you run out the game will end.
